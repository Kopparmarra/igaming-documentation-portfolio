Game Rules / Paytable

Player-facing documentation for the sample slot.

Objective Bet Outcomes Paytable

Objective

The objective is to match colors on the center row. Results are evaluated from left to right after all reels have stopped.

Bet

The selected bet is deducted when the spin starts. If the player balance is lower than the selected bet, the spin is blocked.

Outcomes

OutcomeResult
Three matching symbolsPlayer receives bet multiplied by the symbol payout.
First two reels match, third differsPush. The original bet is returned.
No left-to-right matchNo payout.
